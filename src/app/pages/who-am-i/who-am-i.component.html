Who am I?

After several years working in the pharmaceutical industry, I decided to go for a career change after the birth of my first daughter and got a dietitian diploma in 2013.

Following a tragic family event, I decided in 2014, to become a full-time stay-at-home mom, and then, in 2016, my husband got a job opportunity that brought us to Michigan in the US.

When my second daughter turned 3, I thought it was time for a new career change and that is how I ended up learning reflexology with Barbara Brower and Rebecca Sundeen, who had respectively been practicing for 40 and 20 years, at the Branch Reflexology Institute in Okemos, Michigan.

What makes this course unique is “The Body Talks©” Method approach that links emotions to physical ailments. The idea behind is that each organ or part of the body matches a specific emotion. The use of Bach Flowers comes as a support to the foot or hand reflexology session and helps deal with the corresponding emotions. That is why I also took the Bach Centre level 1 distance learning programme.

After moving back to France in 2023, I decided to settle and open my practice in Castillonnès, the village where my grand-parents used to live.
